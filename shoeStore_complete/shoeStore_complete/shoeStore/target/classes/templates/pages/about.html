Our Story

Footwear is not fashion. It's philosophy.

Founded in 2018, Sole & Co was born from a simple belief — that the shoes you wear shape how you move through the world.

Who we are

Curated with obsession. Delivered with care.

We started Sole & Co in a small apartment in Mumbai with 12 pairs of shoes and a relentless conviction that finding the perfect footwear shouldn't be a chore. Six years later, we've served over 50,000 customers across India.

Every product on our platform is hand-selected by our team of footwear enthusiasts who evaluate comfort, construction, and character before it ever reaches your doorstep.

We partner directly with brands — from global names like Nike and Adidas to artisan Indian cobblers — so you get authentic products at honest prices.
